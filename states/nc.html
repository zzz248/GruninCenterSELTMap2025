⛔ Repealed Low-profit Limited Liability Company (L3C)

North Carolina Limited Liability Company Act

S.B. 439 repealed North Carolina's L3C statute during the 2013-2014 Session. To date, North Carolina is the only state that has abolished the L3C after initially enacting it.

S.B. 439, 2013-2014 Sess. (N.C. 2014)
