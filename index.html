Executive Summary

The Grunin Center for Law and Social Entrepreneurship carries out a portfolio of activities each year that advance our mission to strengthen the community of lawyers and legal institutions engaged in social entrepreneurship, impact investing, and sustainable development. The mix varies from year to year, and this dashboard shows our programming over two academic years.

The dashboard is designed to support reflection and planning. It highlights the breadth of our activities, shows how we engage our three target audiences, and helps us track how our work aligns with our strategic priorities. By providing multiple perspectives, it also gives our leadership and stakeholders a shared tool for understanding impact, identifying opportunities, and guiding the Center's future direction.

How to Use This Dashboard

1

2024-25 & 2025-26 Calendar Views

See the temporal distribution of activities across each academic year. Identify busy periods and opportunities for new programming.

2

View by Audience

Understand how each audience group is served. Shows primary and secondary beneficiaries, helping identify gaps or opportunities for deeper engagement.

3

View by Strategic Pillar

Examine how activities align with our four strategic pillars. Useful for ensuring balanced portfolio and identifying areas for growth.
